Chocolate and banana pancake

    Ingredients:
  • 1 banana
  • 1 2 eggs
  • 10g plain flour
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 10ml almond milk
  • 20g cherry jam
  • chocolate sauce
  • whipped cream
    How to prepare:
  1. Blend all the ingredients together.
  2. Pour the mixture into a frying pan adding some frylight before.
  3. Fry on each side for 5 minutes.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings

Serving: 1 person

Spaghetti bolognese

    Ingredients:
  • 100g pasta
  • 300g turkey thigh mince
  • 5ml olive oil
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • tinned tomatoes
  • tomato pure
  • garlic
  • fresh basil
    How to prepare:
  1. Chop all the veggies and fry on olive oil.
  2. Add turkey mince when you see veggies are soft, fry until gold.
  3. Add tinned tomatoes and tomato pure and cook for an hour.
  4. Cook your pasta.
  5. Serve with fresh basil and cheese if you like.

Serving: 2 people

Salmon ramen

    Ingredients:
  • 1 serving of ramen noodles
  • 200 ml chicken broth
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • fresh garlic
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • mushrooms fried on butter
  • fresh coriander
  • 1 salmon fillet
  • boiled egg
    How to prepare:
  1. Marinate salmon fillet in soy sauce, garlic and honey for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook your noodles as per instructions.
  3. Warm chicken broth up.
  4. Fry salmon fillet on each side for 5 minutes.
  5. Boil the eggs.
  6. Place everything into a bowl and top with coriander.

Serving: 1 person
